Dryvz

Mobile App Connects Driving Trainers With Trainees.

  • Duration: Still In Progress

Developed a user-friendly app with two portals: one for trainees and another for trainers (captains).
Key features include:
- Intuitive UI/UX with multi-language support (Arabic and English)
- Registration and login for trainees and captains
- Trainee portal with home screen displaying all trainings, advanced search, and nearby training sessions based on live location
- Captain portal with session management, reporting, promotion, and payment functionalities
- Real-time chat between trainees and captains
- Live session tracking with review capabilities
- Personal profile management for both users, including account deletion, contact, and terms & conditions
- Integrated Google AdMob for banner and interstitial ads throughout the app
